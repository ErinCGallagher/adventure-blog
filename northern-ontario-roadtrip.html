Northern Ontario Road Trip Itinerary

By Erin Gallagher  -  August 6 - 17, 2020

How to make the best of a Pandemic?

After months of being stuck at home with future out of country travel plans seemingly impossible, my roommate and I decided we needed a real vacation. One that allowed us to really distance ourselves from work since our offices were now our bedroom and living room. 850 sq feet seemed big a few months ago but now it's our enitre world. With COIVD cases decreasing throughout Ontario, we decided it was safe enough to plan a camping roadtrip through Northern Ontario. We found 3 eager friends who wanted to join us and started planning!

This post will cover our itenerary from a high level. Each section will link to a more detailed post about a specific park. We squeezed a lot in but it was all worth it!
